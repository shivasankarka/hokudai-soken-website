Background

It is known that elementary interactions but gravity can be formulated by Quantum Field Theory with a renormalizability. On the other hand, the String Theory is a strong candidate for the Quantum Gravity. And the Super-String Theory becomes a unified theory of "Particles(Matter)" and "Forces(Elementary Interactions)". Furthermore, the Super-String Theory is a very strong analytical method for the system where the other theories are powerless. For instance, we can calculate physical observables exactly in the low energy limit by the string theory in some cases. It is also known that the sum up of the number of state of Black Hole leads to a Statistical Physical description by the Super-String Theory.
